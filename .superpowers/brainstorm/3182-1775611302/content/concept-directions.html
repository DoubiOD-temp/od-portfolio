A. Kinetic Typography Playground

Text that explodes into individual characters, reacts to your mouse with physics (gravity, repulsion, springs), then reassembles. Pretext makes this possible by giving you exact character positions without DOM layout. Think: drag your cursor through a paragraph and watch letters scatter like startled birds, then float back.

B. Text Flowing Around Shapes

Text that wraps around interactive shapes you drag around the canvas. Pretext's variable-width line layout lets each line have a different available width — so text flows around circles, your profile photo, or custom SVG paths in real-time as you move them. This was nearly impossible before without pretext.

C. Text-Particle Metamorphosis

Evolves your existing Experiment page concept: particles that ARE text. A cloud of characters drifts as particles, then on trigger (scroll, click, hover) they smoothly assemble into readable paragraphs using pretext's exact measurements. The transition between chaos and order is the showpiece. Your ASCII art experiment, but leveled up with actual typographic precision.

D. Interactive Magazine Canvas

An entire page rendered on canvas with magazine-quality typography: multi-column text, drop caps, text flowing between regions, pull quotes — all calculated by pretext and painted to canvas. Users can resize columns by dragging, and text reflows instantly. Shows off that pretext can replace the browser's entire text layout engine.
